My Top 3 Favorite Movies

The Thing (1982)

An arctic research team discovers an unidentified lifeform in the ice.

A classic thriller about isolation and mistrust. The practical effects and music are legendary, and really bring to life the tension and distress of the crew's situation.

Tron (1982)

A man inadvertently enters a virtual world of programs.

A hero's story about a man who enters the digital world and helps to liberate the oppressed programs. It's a cool interpretation of what might go on in your computer, and lays the groundwork for the stylish sequel.

The Nightmare Before Christmas (1993)

The Pumpkin King has grown tired of his crown, and wishes for something different.

This animation is amazing. Every character is colorful and fun to listen to. The songs are classics, and I try to watch this every Halloween.
