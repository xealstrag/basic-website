My Favorite Games

Dead Space

A repair team responds to the distress call of the USG Ishimura after it uncovers an alien artifact.

One of the greatest horror games. The setting, enemy design, and sound design are top notch, with unique limb-cutting mechanics and an array of repurposed mining tools at you disposal.

Terraria

Adventure forth, acquire gear, and slay bosses in the world of Terraria.

A solid RPG with different classes, building, and boss-slaying. There's a whole slew of mechanics that have been added over the years, meaning there' always something to do.

Minecraft

A sandbox adventure where the sky's the limit.

A classic game that I've been playing on and off for a long time. The simple focus of mining and crafting has always brought me back. My friends and I usually start a server once a year.
